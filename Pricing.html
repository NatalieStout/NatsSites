Pricing

All website pricing for redesign can be customized to clients individual needs.

Custom Website: $600

The Custom Website package will give you complete control over every aspect of your website. Together we will talk about what you wish to accomplish with your website, the audience you wish to reach, and your customized layout. If you wish, you will have the ability to customize colors, fonts, content, annimations, icons, and any other aspect you want included in your website. The Custom package also includes a mockup, a fully functional prototype, private user testing, and the launch of your new website!

New Website: $500

The New Website package gives you the ability to pick a preexisting template to determine your desiered layout. Together we will determine what you wish to accomplish with your website and the audience you wish to reach. If you wish, you will have the ability to customize colors and content on your website. The New Website package also includes a mockup of your website, testing, and the launch or your new website!

Recycled Website: $300

The Recycled Website package will include a full redesign of your companies current website. Together we will determine what needs to be changed, updated, thrown out, and added to give you the website you have always wanted. This package includes determining goals, audience, and a mockup of the final design. The Recycled Website package will give you the ability to change features, colors, fonts, and content. The Recycled Website package does not include complete layout changes.

Website Touch-ups: $50

The Website Touch-up package includes minor fixes and design changes to your current website. Together we will determine what you want to change and develop a mockup of what you expect in your finished website. The Touch-up package includes content, minor design changes, and debugging if necessary. Pricing for Touch-ups may be increased or decreased based on your websites needs.

Maintainance: $15.00/hour

If you need regularly scheduled maintainance on your website I charge $15.00/hour. Maintainance package specifics can be discussed via email.
